Jung Sung-chan (성찬, Sung Chan), born on September 13, 2001, in Seoul, South Korea, is a South Korean pop singer and a member of the boy group RIIZE. In October 2020, Jung Sung-chan made his official debut with the group NCT by participating in the release of the album "NCT - The 2nd Album RESONANCE Pt.1"; in December of the same year, he won his first music show award since debut with the group on SBS music program "THE SHOW." In March 2021, Jung Sung-chan began hosting the SBS music program "Inkigayo." In October 2021, he released a full album "UNIVERSE" with NCT. In 2023, Jung Sung-chan left NCT and re-debuted with the group RIIZE by releasing the first album "Get A Guitar." In May 2024, RIIZE launched their first fan tour, "2024 RIIZE FAN-CON TOUR 'RIIZING DAY'," starting in Seoul, South Korea, and released their first mini-album "RIIZING" in June. On May 19, 2025, Jung Sung-chan officially made a comeback in the music industry with RIIZE's first full album "ODYSSEY."
